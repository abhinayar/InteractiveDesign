P1 - Visual Translations

Bernie Thoughts

Anayar369 Tumblr

1. Choose a single, regularly updating, text-based feed. This could be a single Twitter account, or a certain blog’s RSS feed, for example. The text from this feed should be relatively short (under 140 characters as a general rule). The focus here is to find an interesting, short, regularly updating text feed that you can use as starting ground. Please note this feed should have 60 items at least in its feed from the start.

2. Create a new account on an image-based social media platform such as Instagram, Tumblr, etc. account specifically for this assignment. This platform should allow you to regularly post an image along with a visible date. 

3. For five out of seven days a week for the next 12 weeks, make a visual translation. A visual translation...

  • is square (recommended 2400px by 2400px)
  • is in either .jpg or .png file format
  • includes one of your chosen feed’s texts
  • includes a separate image, graphic, and/or other visual element
  • should not take longer than 20 minutes to create
  • is posted to your image-based account after creation
