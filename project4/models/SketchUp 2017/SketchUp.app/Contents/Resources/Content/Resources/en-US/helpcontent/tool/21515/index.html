Position Texture Tool

  • Adjust material position on a surface.

Tool Operation

  1. Drag the mouse cursor on the surface to reposition the texture on that surface.
  2. If you want to rotate the tiled image, right/context click on the surface again and select Rotate or Flip.
  3. To finish, right/context click and select Done, or just click outside the texture to exit the Position Texture Tool.

Modifier Keys

  • Shift = Toggle fixed
  • Ctrl = No Snapping
