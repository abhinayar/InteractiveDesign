Walk Tool

  • Walk through (tour) a model.

Tool Operation

  1. Click and hold anywhere in drawing area. A small plus sign (cross hair) is placed at that location.
  2. Move cursor up (forward), down (backward), left (turn left) or right (turn right) to walk. The further from cross hair, the faster you walk.

Modifier Keys

  • Shift = Move up or down instead of forward or backward
  • Ctrl = Run instead of walk
  • Alt = Walk through entities
