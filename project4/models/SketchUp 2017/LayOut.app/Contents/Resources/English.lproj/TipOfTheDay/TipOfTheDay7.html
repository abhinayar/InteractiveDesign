Presenting Your Work

Select the "Start Presentation" menu item from the View menu to enter into presentation mode.

Click the left mouse button (right arrow key) to display the next page. Click the right mouse button (left arrow key) to display the previous page. Click and hold the left mouse button to annotate on a page. Double-click on a model to orbit, pan, or zoom while presenting your work.

Press the "ESC" key to exit from the presentation.
