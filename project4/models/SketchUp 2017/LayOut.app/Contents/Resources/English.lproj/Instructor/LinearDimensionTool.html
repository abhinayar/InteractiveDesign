Dimension Tool
Draw dimensions.

Tool Operation

  1. Click at starting point of dimension.
  2. Move cursor.
  3. Click ending point of dimension.
  4. Move cursor perpendicular to dimension.
  5. Click to fix position of dimension string.

Esc = Cancel operation.

Modifier Keys

  • Alt = Unlock dimension from horizontal, vertical, or perpendicular
  • Ctrl = Hold to toggle leader direction for narrow dimensions
