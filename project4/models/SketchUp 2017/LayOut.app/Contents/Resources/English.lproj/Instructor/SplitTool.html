Split Tool
Create a split between two overlapping shapes at the point where they overlap or split a line.

Tool Operation

  1. Click on one of the intersections between the first and section shape (where their edges overlap).
  2. Click on another intersection between the first and second shape.
  3. (optional) Repeat step 2 for additional intersections between first and second shape (if more than two points on two shapes where shapes intersect).

Esc = Cancel operation.
