i want to apologize
to all the women i have called
pretty.
before i've called them
intelligent or
brave.

i am sorry

i made it sound as though
something as simple as what you're born with
is the most you have to be proud of

when your spirit has
crushed mountains

from now on i will say things like,
you are resilient or,
you are extraordinary.

not
because i don't think you're pretty.
but
because you are so much more than that
